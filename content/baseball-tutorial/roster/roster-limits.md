## Roster Structure

:::league filter=MLB
MLB teams manage multiple roster levels:

- **Active Roster (26 players)** — players who can appear in games
- **40-Man Roster** — the protected roster; all active players plus prospects you want to keep
- **Minor League Roster** — players in your farm system not on the 40-man

:::callout type=important
You can only have 26 players on your active roster and 40 on the 40-man roster. To add someone, you typically need to remove someone else — via trade, release, [DFA]{Designated for Assignment — removing a player from the 40-man roster}, or option to the minors.
:::
:::

:::league filter=College
College teams have a roster limit that varies by division. You manage a single roster — there are no minor leagues. Eligibility rules limit how long players can stay.
:::

## Key Roster Moves

:::compare
| | Move | What It Does | When to Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| | Call Up | Promote from minors to active | Prospect is ready or you need a fill-in |
| | Option | Send to minors (keeps 40-man spot) | Player needs development time |
| | DFA | Remove from 40-man roster | Clearing space for a better player |
| | Release | Completely let go of a player | No longer want them at all |
| | Waiver Claim | Pick up a player another team released | Finding cheap help |
:::

:::league filter=MLB
## Minor League Options

When you option a player to the minors, it uses one of their **option years**. Each player has a limited number of options. Once they're out of options, you can't send them down without risking losing them to another team via waivers.

:::callout type=warning
Track your players' option status carefully. Optioning a player with no remaining options will expose them to waivers — another team can claim them for free.
:::
:::

## The Injured List

When a player gets hurt, you can place them on the [IL]{Injured List — where players go when they are too injured to play}. This frees up their active roster spot so you can call up a replacement. When they recover, you activate them (and must make a corresponding roster move to stay under the limit).
