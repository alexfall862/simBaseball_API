## What Is This?

Welcome to the baseball simulation! You're the General Manager of a baseball franchise. You control the roster, set the gameplan, manage finances, and make every decision that shapes your team's future — from signing free agents to developing minor leaguers.

The sim handles the games. Your job is to put the best team on the field and make smart decisions that lead to championships.

:::callout type=tip
You don't need to understand everything at once. Start by setting your lineup, and explore other areas as you go. This tutorial hub is always here when you have questions.
:::

## How the Sim Works

Each season plays out week by week. Every week is divided into **4 subweeks**, and your team plays games in each one. Between games, players recover stamina, develop their skills, and can get injured.

You set your team's strategy once, and the sim executes it automatically — but you can adjust things anytime.

## What You Control

- **Roster** — who's on your active roster, your bench, and your minor league system
- **Lineup** — batting order and defensive positions
- **Gameplan** — offensive and pitching strategy settings
- **Transactions** — trades, free agent signings, waiver claims
- **Draft / Recruiting** — building for the future through the amateur draft or college recruiting

:::league filter=MLB
As an MLB GM, you also manage a full minor league system, international free agent signings, and arbitration-eligible players.
:::

:::league filter=College
As a college coach, you recruit high school players and junior college transfers, manage eligibility, and build through recruiting classes rather than free agency.
:::
